## What It Does — The Five Core Functions

### 1. Revenue Leak Diagnostic

Identify where revenue is being lost across the business: dropped leads,
slow follow-up, unbilled work, stalled deals, misrouted inquiries, and
unconverted opportunities. The diagnostic produces a ranked list of leaks
with estimated dollar impact and a recommended remediation path.

### 2. Follow-Up Automation

Close the gap between "lead came in" and "lead got a real response."
Automated, founder-branded follow-up sequences that run on the business's
real data — not on generic templates — and escalate to the founder only
when human judgement is actually needed.

### 3. Attribution Dashboard

Show, for every closed deal and every active lead, where it came from,
which channel earned the credit, and what the unit economics look like.
Replaces gut-feel "I think referrals are working" with sourced, auditable
attribution.

### 4. Weekly Executive Intelligence Reporting

A single short weekly brief written for the founder: what changed, what's
leaking, what's working, what needs a decision this week. The report is
generated from the live operational dataset, not from a status meeting.

### 5. Persistent Business Memory

The system remembers clients, past conversations, prior decisions,
recurring objections, channel history, and operational patterns — so the
business stops re-learning the same things every quarter and so new
deployments inherit institutional knowledge.
