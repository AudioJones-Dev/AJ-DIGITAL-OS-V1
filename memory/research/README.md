# Research Memory

## Purpose

Stores source-aware research notes that may inform product, architecture, market, or implementation decisions.

## What Belongs Here

- Research summaries
- Source links and dates
- Confidence levels
- Open questions
- Recommended follow-up

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Unsourced claims treated as decisions
- Copied articles or large quoted passages
- Secrets or client-private data
- Final SOPs or accepted decisions

## Write Access

Human operators and agents may write research notes when sources, confidence, and scope are clear.

## Agent Read Access

Agents may read research through the Memory Router when it is fresh enough and relevant to the task.

## Promotion Path

Accepted conclusions move to `memory/decisions/`. Repeatable procedures move to `memory/sops/`. Source metadata should be preserved in canonical memory.
